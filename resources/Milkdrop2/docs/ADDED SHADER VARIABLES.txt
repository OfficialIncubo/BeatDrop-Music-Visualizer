Mouse controls:
mouse_x - retrieves the X position of mouse
mouse_y - retrieves the Y position of mouse
mouse_pos - retrieves the XY position of mouse
mouse_hold - retrieves if the left or right button of the mouse is held or not
mouse_click - retrieves if the left or right button of the mouse is clicked (button up) or not

You can also use:
mouse.x - retrieves the X position of mouse
mouse.y - retrieves the Y position of mouse
mouse.z - retrieves if the left or right button of the mouse is held or not
mouse.w - retrieves if the left or right button of the mouse is clicked (button up) or not
